- clinical_catalog focuses on category_id and type. master_medicines focuses on category, unit, prices.
- inventory has doctor_id and tracks stock. doctor_service_settings maps global catalog item to doctor with custom_price.
- catalog_versions and catalog_audit_logs support both 'clinical' and 'pharmacy' in their entity_type enums.
- Missing snapshot_json in promotion_requests (table may not even exist/be migrated).
